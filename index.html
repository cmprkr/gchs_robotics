GOLDEN GEARS BLOG

Check out the blog of the Guerin Catholic High School Robotics Team, proudly known as the Golden Gears! This space is your ultimate portal to dive deep into the exciting world of robotics, where innovation meets passion. Here, you'll find a treasure trove of updates, stories, and insights that showcase the incredible journey of our team.

We also share tips for aspiring roboticists, reflections from our team members about their experiences, and insights into the cutting-edge technology we're exploring. Whether you're a student interested in joining the robotics field, a parent supporting your child's technological pursuits, or just a fan of robotics, there's something here for everyone.

COMING SOON: GUERIN CATHOLIC JOINS FIRST ROBOTICS

At Guerin Catholic High School, we are thrilled to be a part of FIRST Robotics for the 2024-25 season, the international high school robotics competition that combines the excitement of sport with the rigors of science and technology. With a motto of "For Inspiration and Recognition of Science and Technology" (FIRST), this program offers an unparalleled experience for students to become science and technology leaders.

FIRST Robotics Competition (FRC) is a unique varsity Sport for the Mindâ„¢ designed to help young people discover how interesting and rewarding the life of engineers and researchers can be. It challenges teams of high school students and their mentors to design and build a robot in a six-week timeframe using a standard "kit of parts" and a common set of rules. Teams then compete in a series of games that test the robot's capabilities, the team's strategy, and their ability to work together under pressure.
